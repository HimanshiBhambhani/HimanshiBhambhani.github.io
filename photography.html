I love travelling and photography.So, here I'm sharing my travelling stories through the photographs I've clicked.

Enjoy!!

  • Have a look!

Photograph1

A beautiful and soothing scene of the rainy season of an old school cafetaria of the old library of my college Thapar University. In the exam times of my first year, me and my friends used to study together at the library, and have lots of fun.

Photograph2

I belong to one of the most beautiful cities of India, Udaipur- The City of Lakes. You can never get over this city ever. The lakes are so beautiful and calming. This is a picture of the most visited lake of Udaipur-'Fateh Sagar'.

Photograph3

Every leaf speaks bliss to me and so this is my favorite picture out of all. This was also taken in my college.

Photograph4

Sunsets are so beautiful that they almost seem as if we are looking through the gates of heaven. This is a picture of sun setting over the 'Fateh Sagar Lake' Udaipur.

Photograph5

This is a picture of one of the corriors of my college. This is an example of infinite photography in which it seems that the corridor is extending infinitely.

Photograph6

So, as I mentioned before that I love travelling ,but restricted for now due to Covid-19 :( . This is a picture of a beautiful river in Kasol,Manali. It was the most adventerous and exciting trip ever that I had with my best friend of that time. It was about two girls travelling alone, making friends and exploring. :D

Photograph7

Nothing is more beautiful than a smile that has struggled through years and tears. A picture that can bring a smile on your at any time of the day. This picture was taken in a very famous art and crafts festival that takes place in Udaipur in the month of December known as Shilgram Utsav.

Photograph8

Also a picture of the same festival that I mentioned in the previous picture. People from different states perform their local dances here, so this is one of those dances.
